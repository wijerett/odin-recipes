CrabCakes

Description

Delicious and crispy fresh Jumbo lump crab cakes, served with a bed of kale and arugula and lemon vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • Jumbo lump crab meat
  • Bread crumbs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Egg
Steps
  1. Mix all ingredients in a small mixing bowl
  2. Form into balls medium size and place firmly onto greased baking sheet smashing as you place it
  3. Bake for 35 minutes at 375 degrees
