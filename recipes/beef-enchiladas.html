Beef-Enchiladas

Description

This a very savory dish including choice beef rolled in homemade flour tortillas, coated in a spicy red tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla dough
  • Red Sauce
  • Choice ground beef
  • Mozzarella and Colby Jack cheese shredded
Steps
  1. Roll dough into flat workable size, about 1/4 inch thick
  2. Add meat to each tortilla and roll lightly and place into well greased, oven safe baking dish
  3. Top with sauce and blend of mozzarella and colby jack cheeses
  4. Bake for 40 minutes at 400 degrees and enjoy!
