Meatloaf

Description

Delicious mixture of savory meat and sweet tomato sauce topping

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Salt
  • Mustard
  • Eggs
Steps
  1. Mix meat and all ingredients in a small mixing bowl
  2. Form into a large mound shape in a finely greased baking dish
  3. Top with ketchup and brown sugar mix, bake and baste every 20 minutes
